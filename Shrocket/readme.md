# Made with

* Affinity Designer
* [Gingerbread](https://gingerbread.wntr.dev/index.html)
* KiCad
    * Blinkyparts custom footprint library (submodule) [https://codeberg.org/blinkyparts/blinkyparts.pretty](https://codeberg.org/blinkyparts/blinkyparts.pretty)

# Soldering tutorial

Prepare the parts
![Photo](photo-tutorial/solder_tutorial_23.webp)

Add solder to the long pad of the bottom LED
![Photo](photo-tutorial/solder_tutorial_44.webp)

Bend the legs of one LED to look like this
![Photo](photo-tutorial/solder_tutorial_48.webp)

Solder one LED at the bottom. Solder the long leg to the long pad with the + sign
![Photo](photo-tutorial/solder_tutorial_02.webp)

Should look like this
![Photo](photo-tutorial/solder_tutorial_04.webp)

Add solder to the long pads of the 3 top LEDs
![Photo](photo-tutorial/solder_tutorial_47.webp)

Solder the remaining 3 LEDs to the 3 pads on top.
![Photo](photo-tutorial/solder_tutorial_05.webp)

Should look like this
![Photo](photo-tutorial/solder_tutorial_06.webp)

Snip the excess legs
![Photo](photo-tutorial/solder_tutorial_07.webp)

Should look like this
![Photo](photo-tutorial/solder_tutorial_08.webp)

Clip the legs of the 3 top LEDs.
![Photo](photo-tutorial/solder_tutorial_10.webp)

Bend the legs of the switch so they touch the table.
![Photo](photo-tutorial/solder_tutorial_14.webp)

Solder one leg of the switch
![Photo](photo-tutorial/solder_tutorial_15.webp)

Solder the other leg of the switch
![Photo](photo-tutorial/solder_tutorial_16.webp)

Add solder to the left pad of the battery holder.
![Photo](photo-tutorial/solder_tutorial_17.webp)

Solder the left leg of the battery holder
![Photo](photo-tutorial/solder_tutorial_18.webp)

Finished with soldering
![Photo](photo-tutorial/solder_tutorial_19.webp)

Add the battery. Careful: the left arms must go on top of the battery, and the longer right arms below.
![Photo](photo-tutorial/solder_tutorial_21.webp)

It should look like this. Check again that the 2 small arms on the left side of the battery holder touch the battery from the top.
![Photo](photo-tutorial/solder_tutorial_22.webp)

Finished with soldering
![Photo](photo-tutorial/solder_tutorial_24.webp)

Switch it on and check if all LEDs light up.
![Photo](photo-tutorial/solder_tutorial_25.webp)

They should switch from red to green
![Photo](photo-tutorial/solder_tutorial_26.webp)

Add hot glue to the LEDs
![Photo](photo-tutorial/solder_tutorial_28.webp)

Only a small drop
![Photo](photo-tutorial/solder_tutorial_31.webp)

A bit of hot glue at the bottom LED
![Photo](photo-tutorial/solder_tutorial_41.webp)

Finished. Now decorate the front side with glitter if you want to.
![Photo](photo-tutorial/solder_tutorial_42.webp)
