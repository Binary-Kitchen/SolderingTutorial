# Hearbeat - THT
Small soldering kit in form of an heart. A flashing (beating) LED is located in the center of the beating heart.

<img src="manual/images/thumbnail.jpg" width=400px alt="Heartbeat THT">

- Status: **Complete**
- Difficulty: **2/5**
